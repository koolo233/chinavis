ChinaVis 2021 Arts Program Student Competition

Shortlist

We received a total of 296 works submitted by 995 people from 85 universities around the world on the 2 themes this year. After double-blind review, a total of 36 excellent works were shortlisted, with a shortlisting ratio of 12.16%. Congratulations to the shortlisted teachers and students! At the same time, thank you 29 review experts for their hard work! The award ceremony will be held during the conference. At least one person from the winning team is required to register for the conference and participate in the award ceremony.
