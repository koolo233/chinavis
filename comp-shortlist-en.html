China VISAP'21 Student Competition

Shortlists

This year's China VISAP Student Competition received a total of 296 submissions from 85 universities around the world for the two themes: 1. Bridge (China VISAP’21 theme), and 2. The Air We Breathe (dataset designated). After the double-blind evaluation, 36 outstanding works were shortlisted, with approximately an acceptance rate of 12.16%. The awarding ceremony will be held during the ChinaVis 2021 Conference. At least one member of each team is required to register for the Conference and attend the awarding ceremony. The registration link can be accessed here: http://chinavis.org/2021/english/register_en.html

Many congratulations to all students and their mentors! In addition, we’d like to express our sincere gratitude towards our 29 judges for their expertise and excellent work!
